Getting Started

4 questions

It's simple! We provide each unit with a bin to place outside their door. Residents place their trash in the bin by 8 PM on collection nights, and our uniformed valets collect it during our evening window (8 PM - midnight). We transport all trash to your property's dumpster, so residents never have to make that walk.

We proudly serve multifamily communities throughout Florida, including:

  • Central Florida: Orlando, Winter Park, Kissimmee, Sanford
  • Tampa Bay: Tampa, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Brandon
  • South Florida: Miami, Fort Lauderdale, West Palm Beach, Boca Raton
  • Northeast: Jacksonville, St. Augustine, Gainesville

Use our service area checker or contact us to confirm coverage.

We can typically begin service within 7-14 days of signing your agreement. This includes:

  • Property walkthrough and route planning
  • Bin delivery to all units
  • Resident notification and welcome materials

Need it faster? We offer expedited setup for an additional fee.

We work with properties of all sizes! From boutique communities with 20 units to large complexes with 500+ units. Our pricing scales based on unit count, and we offer volume discounts for larger properties.

For Residents

4 questions

Place trash in the provided bin and set it outside your door by 8 PM on collection nights. Make sure the lid closes completely and bring the empty bin back inside after collection.

Allowed: Bagged kitchen waste, small boxes (broken down), general household items that fit with lid closed.

NOT Allowed: Hazardous materials, liquids, sharp objects (unless safely contained), extremely heavy items.

Contact your property management office. Replacement bins are typically delivered within 48 hours and are free for normal wear and tear.

Valet trash is typically a community-wide amenity. Individual opt-outs are generally not available since it's included in rent/fees. If you have concerns, speak with your property manager.
