Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our doorstep trash valet service

Getting Started

It's simple! We provide each unit with a bin to place outside their door. Residents place their trash in the bin by 8 PM, and our uniformed valets collect it during our evening collection window (typically 8 PM - 12 AM). We transport all trash to your property's main dumpster, so residents never have to make that walk themselves.

We proudly serve multifamily communities throughout Florida, including Orlando, Tampa, Jacksonville, Miami, Fort Lauderdale, West Palm Beach, St. Petersburg, and Clearwater. If you're not sure if we service your area, use our ZIP code checker or contact us directly.

We can typically begin service within 7-14 days of signing your agreement. This includes time for a property walkthrough, bin delivery to all units, and resident notification. For properties that need faster implementation, we offer expedited setup for an additional fee.

We work with properties of all sizes, from boutique communities with 20 units to large complexes with 500+ units. Our pricing scales based on unit count, and we offer volume discounts for larger properties. Every community deserves premium service!

Pricing & Billing

Our pricing starts at $10 per unit per month for our Essential plan (3 nights/week). Our most popular Standard plan is $12.50/unit/month for 5 nights/week, and Premium is $15/unit/month for 7 nights/week plus extra features. We offer volume discounts for larger properties—up to 15% off for communities with 200+ units. Check our pricing page or use our quote calculator for exact pricing.

No hidden fees, ever. Your quoted price includes bins, regular bin cleaning, collection on scheduled nights, and transport to your dumpster. The only additional charges would be optional add-ons like recycling, bulk removal, or special services that you request. We believe in transparent pricing.

We offer flexible contract terms starting at 12 months, with discounts available for longer commitments. But here's what sets us apart: our 90-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied within the first 90 days, you can cancel with no penalties. We're confident you'll love our service.

We offer multiple payment options for your convenience: ACH bank transfer (preferred), credit card, or check. Invoices are sent on the 1st of each month with payment due by the 15th. We can also accommodate custom billing cycles to match your property's accounting schedule.
